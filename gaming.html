Gaming

The Game Room Wants You!

The ConGlomeration Game Room is open and staffed 24 hours a day during the convention – the rest is up to you.

Our Game Room is made possible by the fans who love games. We need your help with running those games. If you are interested in running any tabletop game – boardgame, roleplaying game, card game, or miniature game – please contact the gaming staff to get the ball rolling.

A list of games confirmed for ConGlomeration 2016 will be posted below. In all games, players sign up at the convention on a first come, first served basis. Games requiring advance interest will be clearly noted.

Conglomeration offers special thanks to:

  • Evil Beagle Games
